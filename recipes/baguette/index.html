Ingredients

  • 2 c warm water (about 110℉)
  • 1 T active dry yeast
  • 2½ t sugar
  • 5 - 6 c bread flour
  • 2½ t salt
  • 1 t oil

Instructions

  • 01
    In a small bowl, combine water, yeast, and sugar. Let sit for 5 minutes.
  • 02
    In a stand mixer, place 2 cups of flour with the 2 ½ teaspoon salt and yeast mixture. Combine with dough hook. Add in ½ cup of the remaining flour at a time until the dough is smooth but not sticky. Rub the olive oil around the dough and let rest for 15 minutes.
  • 03
    Turn the dough onto a flour surface and divide into 3 pieces. Roll one piece into a rectangle. Starting from the long side, roll the dough into a cylinder. Turn both ends in and pinch the seams closed. Round the edges and place onto a baking sheet. Repeat with the second and third dough balls. Let rise for 45 minutes. Make three diagonal cuts across the top of each loaf.
  • 04
    Preheat the oven to 400℉. Bake for 20 minutes or until an internal temp of 190℉.
