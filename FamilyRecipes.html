Preparation
  1. In a large bowl, whisk together ¾ cup sugar, ¼ cup cornstarch and a pinch of salt. Add 4 egg yolks and whisk until smooth.
  2. In a medium saucepan bring 2 cups milk to just a boil.
  3. Gradually add hot milk in a steady steam to the egg mixture, whisking constantly until all of the milk is incorporated.
    Pour mixture back into saucepan and bring to a boil while whisking constantly until mixture is thick and pudding-like
    consistency.
  4. Take the saucepan off the heat. Add in 2 teaspoons of vanilla and 4 tablespoons of butter and whisk until combined.
  5. Cover with plastic and chill in the fridge for at least 1 hour.
